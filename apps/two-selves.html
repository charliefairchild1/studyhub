Two Selves, One Substrate

Same nodes. Same information. Two self-models — different access (walls), attention, and drive. Ask them the same thing and watch what each can, and can't, say.
The substrate (memory nodes) is identical and shared — only the self-config differs. Lit = the self attended to it; walled = the node exists but this self can't reach it. Same data, two views.

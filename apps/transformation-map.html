The Transformation Map

Don't start from tickers — start from the world. Find the directions where potential is converting into transformation fastest, relative to what's dissipated, then find the companies that realize that potential. Top-down by trajectory.
The PTO lens, per direction: Potential = how big the latent shift is · Transformation = how fast it's actually happening now · Dissipation = risk + how much is already priced in (a great trend bought too dear pays poorly). Net score rewards high potential × transformation, penalized by dissipation. Tap a card for the realizers.
⚠ A thinking framework, not stock picks or advice. Tickers are examples to research. The safest way to bet a theme (rather than one company) is usually a low-cost ETF — listed per direction. Valuations move; a right theme with over-loved stocks can still lose.

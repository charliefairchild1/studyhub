What this is

An epidemic is PEP's four primitives applied to disease:
• Weighted graph: population = nodes, social contacts = edges, contact strength = weight
• Spreading activation: virus propagates along edges, probability scales with edge weight
• State modulation: vaccination / masks / distance reduce transmission
• Opacity / haze: infected → recovered over time (activation fades)

Nothing exotic. Same four tools as every other PEP demo. Pointed here.

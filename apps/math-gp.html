Why PEP cares about uncertainty. A point predictor that confidently extrapolates beyond its training data is dangerous. A GP collapses uncertainty where you've observed and reverts to the prior elsewhere. That's exactly what retrieval should do — high confidence near anchored memories, low confidence in the space between them. Tied to 📈 predictor (uncertainty-aware twin) and Β Bayes (function-space generalization).

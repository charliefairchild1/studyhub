Why PEP cares. Real retrieval graphs grow scale-free — popular concepts attract more associations, popular pages get more inbound edges. That's why Ⅿ PageRank works (high-degree nodes carry most stationary mass) and why ⟳ spreading can reach the whole graph in O(log N) steps from any seed (small-world property).

Power-law exponent γ ≈ 3 is theoretical for pure Barabási-Albert. Smaller γ → fatter tail → more dominance by hubs.

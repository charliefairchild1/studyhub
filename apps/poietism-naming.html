◈ My read (broadened)

You’re right — a name tied to dice would misname the thing. The spine of PEP + PTO is directed becoming: making order out of potential, against dissipation. So the strongest names plant transformation / making / the path, and let "under uncertainty" be a clause, not the title. Three I’d put on a cover:

Poietism — from poiesis, a bringing-forth into being. The broadest name for the whole view: the good is to make — to turn potential into what wasn’t there. My #1.

Syntropism — from syntropy, the drive toward order (the opposite of entropy). Names PTO’s exact value — constructive transformation against dissipation — in one scientific word.

Entelechism — from Aristotle’s entelecheia, potential carrying its own end into actuality. This is Potential → Transformation → Output, named.

If you’d rather foreground acting well under uncertainty without reducing to gambling, Kairism (the decisive moment) does that. The dice-names — Kybeism, Clinamism — are now filed as one facet, not the flag.

What each root literally carries: 🌱 becoming ⚙️ transform 🔨 construct 🧭 direction 🎲 uncertainty

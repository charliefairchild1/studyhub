PEP connection. MCMC is how you sample a posterior when you can only evaluate it up to a constant — which is the default situation for any Bayes problem PEP cares about. Rejecting a step is the residual saying "that direction is worse — don't go." Accept-ratio = local quality signal. No gradient, no smoothness required. Works when gradient descent can't.

Try this

σ very small: high acceptance, chain stuck near start. Covariance underestimated.
σ very large: low acceptance, chain wastes most proposals.
Sweet spot ≈ 23.4%: classic optimal acceptance for high-dim Gaussians.
Funnel: notoriously hard — gets stuck in neck.

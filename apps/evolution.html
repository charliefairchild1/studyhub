Five PEP primitives, one canvas. Ancestry edges = weighted graph. Drift along lineage = spreading. Fitness = prediction of environment; selection = residual update. Stabilizing selection: cold + low mutation + high selection power. Species converge on the single peak.
Punctuated equilibrium: stable for a while, then switch climate. Watch mass extinction, then rapid radiation into the new peak.
Bimodal: two peaks compete. Slight mutation drift decides which lineage wins.

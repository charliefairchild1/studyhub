Why PEP cares. Primitive 3 (predictor + residual) has a hard floor: you cannot compress a source below its entropy. Residual magnitude → H(X) in the limit. A perfect predictor of a fair coin still produces 1 bit of residual per flip. High-entropy domains have irreducible surprise; that's why novelty detectors matter even after training saturates.

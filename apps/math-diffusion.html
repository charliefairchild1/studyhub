Einstein's relation. For a simple random walk with step τ, spacing h, ⟨x²⟩ = 2D t with D = h²/(2τ). The left canvas is the right canvas, one sample at a time. This is the formal statement that spreading activation is diffusion with a discrete stencil.

Why it matters for PEP. The predictable-envelope of where activation will be after k steps is exactly the Gaussian ρ(x, t) = e^(-x²/(4Dt)) / √(4πDt). That envelope is what the engine uses to decide the reach horizon of a query.

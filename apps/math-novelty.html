Novelty

Each symbol has a running familiarity. Novelty = 1 − familiarity. Above threshold, the flag fires and the symbol glows. Silence decays familiarity — you get your sensitivity back.

Familiarity per symbol

PEP primitive 3. Novelty is the residual made useful. The familiarity model is a one-step predictor over symbol frequency; its error on the current symbol is the novelty score. Habituation = learning reduces error. Dishabituation = decay restores error. Axona uses this to gate attention; Vectora Watch uses it to flag anomalous queries; Atria uses it to surface outlier matches.

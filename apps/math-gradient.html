PEP primitive 3. The predictor's weight update under MSE loss is SGD step. Momentum = trust your recent gradient more. Adam = auto-scale by coordinate volatility. All three are residual-driven — they need the loss gradient to act. No residual, no learning. Which optimizer wins depends on the loss shape, which depends on the data — there is no free lunch.

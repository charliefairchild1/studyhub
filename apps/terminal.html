Terminal

A live shell to your Mac, from your phone — run claude, edit code, anything. Your Mac must be on and running the terminal server.

Saved on this device. Paste the URL your Mac printed when you started the server.

Start it on your Mac (one command)

In a terminal on your Mac, run:

~/projects/studyhub/terminal-server.sh

It prints a URL and a password. Paste the URL above, tap Open, and log in with user + that password. Leave that Mac terminal open — closing it ends the session.

⚠ Security: this URL is a door to your computer's shell. Anyone who has both the URL and password can run commands as you. Keep them private, and stop the server (Ctrl-C in that Mac window) when you're done. The password is random each start.

Then, on your phone

  1. Tap Open terminal above.
  2. Log in: user / the printed password.
  3. You're in your Mac's shell. Type claude to start coding with your Max plan.

Tip: for a terminal that works from anywhere without your Mac needing a public tunnel, a dedicated SSH app (Blink, Termius) over Tailscale is the sturdier long-term setup — ask and I'll wire that instead.

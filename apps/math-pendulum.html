Why we keep bringing this up. Primitive 3 (predictor + residual) has a finite horizon in any chaotic system. No matter how precise your model, two trajectories that agree on the visible state will diverge. For PEP retrieval this is fine — we predict relevance distributions, not exact states. For agents that depend on exact forecasting (weather > 2 weeks, market > minutes, brain state > tens of seconds), chaos is the wall.
